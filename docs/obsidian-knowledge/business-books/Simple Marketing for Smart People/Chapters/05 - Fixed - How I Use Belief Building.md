## Summary

- Without Belief Building
	- discounting or promotin or creating urgency is meaningless
	- drowning them in endless how to tutorials
	- creating mountains of free content doesn’t necessarily help the prospect and may actually hurt you.
	- writing down every random content idea I could think of, ==which is like throwing spaghetti at the wall. ^ref-50693

- marketing is highly divergent
	- in contrast, the Belief Building question is convergent. 
	- It brings order by reducing the number of things you’re doing rather than expanding them. ^ref-1482


- when create content or go on someone's media, plug the holes in people’s belief systems. 
	- It’s like getting a little cork and putting it in one of those leaks.
